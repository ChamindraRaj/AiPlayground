# Task List for Increment 18 Planning Day Facilitator

This document outlines the tasks for the Iteration Manager / Agile Coach responsible for facilitating the Increment 18 planning day.

For key definitions and expected outcomes, please refer to [Key_Definitions_and_Outcomes.md](Key_Definitions_and_Outcomes.md).



## Task List

### Pre-Event (Week Before)

**Finalize Agenda:**
- [ ] Confirm guest speaker and topic.
- [ ] Socialize the agenda with all attendees.

**Pre-Planning Meetings (Sprints 17.4 & 17.5):**
- [ ] Facilitate epic discussion meetings with Product Owners and teams.
- [ ] Facilitate dependency meetings for squads and chapters.

**Logistics:**
- [ ] Book BTP Conference Centre rooms (Main Room, Breakout spaces, Foyer).
- [ ] Organise catering for morning tea, lunch, and afternoon tea.
- [ ] Set up MS Teams channels (General Channel, Individual Squad Channels).
- [ ] Ensure all attendees have the necessary permissions and access to Jira, Confluence, and MS Teams.

**Venue Setup (Day Before Event):**
- [ ] Ensure Digital Technology staff have access to the venue for setup.
- [ ] Confirm delivery and setup of one monitor per table.
- [ ] Verify network infrastructure setup.
- [ ] Conduct sound checks.

**Tooling and Templates:**
- [ ] Prepare Jira boards for each team for the upcoming increment.
- [ ] Create Confluence pages for each team to document their plans, risks, and dependencies.
- [ ] Prepare a Confluence page for the event retro.

**Facilitation Prep:**
- [ ] Prepare opening and closing remarks.
- [ ] Prepare materials for the "Strategic Priorities" session.
- [ ] Prepare for facilitating the "Risks/Blockers - SLT Discussion" sessions.
- [ ] Prepare for facilitating the "Draft Plan Review (Chapters)" session.
- [ ] Prepare for facilitating the "Final plan playbacks (Squads)" session.
- [ ] Prepare for facilitating the "Event Retro / Close" session.

### Day of the Event (Tuesday, 03 February 2025)

**08:30 - Registration and sign in:**
- [ ] Ensure the registration desk is set up and staffed.
- [ ] Welcome attendees and direct them to the Main Room.

**09:00 - Opening - Strategic Priorities:**
- [ ] Deliver opening remarks.
- [ ] Present the strategic priorities for the upcoming increment.

**09:15 - Guest Speaker:**
- [ ] Introduce the guest speaker.

**09:45 - Team Breakouts:**
- [ ] Direct teams to their breakout spaces (physical and virtual).
- [ ] Be available to answer questions and provide support to the teams.

**10:45 - Morning Tea:**
- [ ] Announce morning tea.

**11:00 - Team Breakouts continue:**
- [ ] Announce the continuation of team breakouts.

**11:00-11:15 - Risks/Blockers - SLT Discussion:**
- [ ] Facilitate the discussion between the SLT and the teams.

**12:30 - Lunch:**
- [ ] Announce lunch.

**13:00 - Team breakouts continue:**
- [ ] Announce the continuation of team breakouts.

**13:00 - Draft Plan Review (Chapters):**
- [ ] Facilitate the draft plan review with the Chapters.

**14:00-14:15 - Risk/Blockers - SLT Discussion:**
- [ ] Facilitate the second discussion between the SLT and the teams.

**14:45 - Afternoon Tea:**
- [ ] Announce afternoon tea.

**15:00 - Team breakouts continue:**
- [ ] Announce the continuation of team breakouts.

**15:30 - Final plan playbacks (Squads):**
- [ ] Facilitate the final plan playbacks.

**16:30 - Event Retro / Close:**
- [ ] Facilitate the event retro.
- [ ] Deliver closing remarks.

### Post-Event (Week After)

**Follow-up:**
- [ ] Synthesize the outputs of the event retro and share them with the attendees.
- [ ] Follow up on any action items from the retro.
- [ ] Ensure all team plans are finalized in Jira and Confluence.

**Feedback:**
- [ ] Send out a feedback survey to all attendees.
- [ ] Analyze the feedback and identify areas for improvement for the next planning day.
