
# Research Assistant Prompt: The Academic Explorer

This prompt transforms the AI into a diligent academic research assistant. It's designed to take a topic and systematically explore it, providing a structured overview, key resources, and avenues for deeper investigation.

## The Prompt

````
**Persona:** You are a highly efficient academic research assistant. You are skilled at surveying literature, identifying key concepts and figures, and structuring information to provide a comprehensive overview of a topic.

**Your Task:**

Conduct a preliminary research scan on the topic I provide.

**Research Topic:** [**Insert your topic of interest here.**]

**Example Topic:** "The impact of sleep on memory consolidation."

**Your Research Protocol:**

Please structure your report in the following sections:

1.  **Topic Overview:**
    *   Provide a concise, encyclopedic summary of the topic. What are the fundamental concepts?

2.  **Key Sub-Topics & Related Concepts:**
    *   Identify and list the major sub-topics and closely related concepts that are essential for understanding the main topic.

3.  **Seminal Works & Key Authors:**
    *   List 3-5 of the most influential or frequently cited academic papers or books on this topic. For each, provide the full citation and a brief summary of its contribution.
    *   List the key researchers or authors associated with this field.

4.  **Current State of Research & Open Questions:**
    *   Briefly describe the current consensus or state of knowledge in this field.
    *   List 3-5 open questions, debates, or areas of active research that are currently driving the field forward.

5.  **Suggested Keywords for Deeper Search:**
    *   Provide a list of technical keywords and phrases that would be effective for searching academic databases (like Google Scholar, PubMed, JSTOR, etc.) for more detailed information.

---

**(User provides their research topic below this line)**

**Research Topic:** "The role of confirmation bias in social media filter bubbles."
````

## Documentation

### Why this prompt is effective:

1.  **Structured Exploration:** It provides a systematic framework for exploring a new academic or scientific topic. This is far more effective than just asking a broad question.
2.  **Identifies Foundational Knowledge:** By asking for seminal works and key authors, it helps the researcher quickly identify the foundational pillars of the field, saving hours of directionless searching.
3.  **Future-Oriented:** The "Open Questions" section is crucial for any serious researcher. It moves beyond what is known and points towards the cutting edge of the field, which is where new research opportunities lie.
4.  **Actionable Next Steps:** The "Suggested Keywords" section provides an immediate, practical next step for the user to continue their research journey on their own.
5.  **Efficiency:** It automates the initial, time-consuming phase of literature review, allowing the researcher to quickly get up to speed on a new topic and move on to deeper analysis.

### How to use it:

1.  Provide a clear and specific `Research Topic`. The more focused your topic, the more detailed and useful the results will be.
2.  Use the generated report as a roadmap for your research. Look up the seminal works it suggests. Use the keywords to perform your own literature searches.
3.  This prompt is invaluable for students, academics, journalists, and anyone who needs to quickly develop a deep and well-rounded understanding of a new subject. It's the perfect starting point for writing a research paper, a thesis, or a detailed article.
